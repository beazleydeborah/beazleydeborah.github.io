His Name Is Wonderful
=
His name is Wonderful,
His name is Wonderful;
His name is Wonderful, Jesus, my Lord.
He is the Mighty King,
Master of everything;
His name is Wonderful, Jesus, my Lord.
=
He's the great Shepherd,
The Rock of all Ages.
Almighty God is He.
=
Bow down before him, Love and adore him;
His name is Wonderful, Jesus, my Lord.
