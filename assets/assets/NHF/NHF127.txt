title:Da’ Dil Bee Táán’disgiz?
subtitle:Are You Washed in the Blood?
language:navajo
=
Jesus daats’í néinikaahgo bee ííldiil, 
Da’bidil bee tá náni’disgiz? 
Yee jooba’iísh k’ad éí yinídláa dooleel,
=

Yee jooba’go éí yee táá’ágis, Ne’iina’ásh
