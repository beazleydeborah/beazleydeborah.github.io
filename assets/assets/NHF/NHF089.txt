title:Díí Jį́ Áhoodzaagoshą’?
subtitle:What If It Were Today?
language:navajo
=
Jesus Christ éí nihaa nídooodááł. 
Díí jį́ níyáagoshą’?
Yee a yóó’át’éii éí yił doogááł. 
Díí jį́ níyáagoshą’?
Kéyah nidahonees’ą́ą́dę́ę́’ 
Nááyiis’nii’go bíí’ daazlį́į’ii 
Nikéídoo’ish yiniyé doogááł. 
Díí jį́ nádzáagoshą’?
=
Baa hózhǫ́ǫgo Shijéí bii’ hwiidoo’aał,
Baa ha’niihgo Naat’áanii nilį́į doo,
Baa ha’niihgo Bá hasht’edahohłééh,
Baa ha’niihgo Jesus nihaa doogááł.
=
Chahałheeł binaanish nidoot’ih. 
Díí jį́, óonééh laanaa!
Yínííł ti’ ayoołnííh ádin doo. 
Dííjį́ óonééh laanaa!
Áko Christ yii’ daneeznánę́ę 
Wódahgo Christ yidááh dookah.
Hahgo lá éidí deidiiltsééł lá? 
Díí jį́ nádzáagoshą’?
=
Baa hózhǫ́ǫgo Shijéí bii’ hwiidoo’aał,
Baa ha’niihgo Naat’áanii nilį́į doo,
Baa ha’niihgo Bá hasht’edahohłééh,
Baa ha’niihgo Jesus nihaa doogááł.
=
T’áá’aaníígóó ádaniit’éego 
Daats’í nihaa doogááł?
Baa nihił dahózhǫ́ǫgo daats’í, 
Díí jį́ nihaa doogááł?
Nádáahgo bee bééhózinii 
T’óó’ahayóí dahólǫ́ k’ad.
Yéego hada’ísídół’į́į́, 
T’áádíí jį́da doogááł.
=
