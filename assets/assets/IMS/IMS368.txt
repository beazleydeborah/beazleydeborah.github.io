title:Come Thou Fount
subtitle:w/Navajo
language:navajo
=
Tó háálįijį’ ahohkáahgo
Baa hózho-go dahohtaał,
Bijooba’ doo nineełnéehii
Baa ha’ niihgo dahohtaał,
Yá’ąąshdi yee dahataałii,
Éí bee shiníítįił dooleeł,
Ayóó’óó’ní yee hááshííłti,
Áko éí bee baa hashniih.
=
Come Thou fount of every blessing
Tune my heart to sing Thy grace
Streams of mercy never ceasing
Call for songs of loudest praise
Teach me some melodious sonnet
Sung by flaming tongues above
Praise the mount I'm fixed upon it
Mount of Thy redeeming love
=
ShiTaa’ shíká íínílwodgo
Dííjiijį’ shił nííní’áázh,
Íinínízinii bik’ehgo
Yá’át’éehgo naa deeshááł,
God bits’ąájį’ yóó’ííyáago
Jesus bee shénáhoosdzin,
Báhádzidii yik’eh deesdliį́’,
Bidił t’éí yee nááshiis’nii’.
=
Here I raise my Ebenezer
Hither by Thy help I come
And I hope by Thy good pleasure
Safely to arrive at home
Jesus sought me when a stranger
Wandering from the fold of God
He to rescue me from danger
Interposed His precious blood
=
Nijooba’ bee shąą háíníł‘á,
Áko t’óó níí’í’diishyaa
Bee ayóó’á shííní’níinii
Éí bee shejéí níí’ nili.
Áko doo yóó’adeesháał da,
Doo shidziil dago éí bąą,
Shijéí t’áá’át’é ni dooleeł,
Yá’ąąshdi yah an'deeshdááł.
=
O to grace how great a debtor
Daily I'm constrained to be
Let Thy goodness like a fetter
Bind my wandring heart to Thee
Prone to wander Lord I feel it
Prone to leave the God I love
Here's my heart Lord , take and seal it
Seal it for Thy courts above
