title:Diyin, Diyinii God
subtitle:Holy, Holy, Holy
language:navajo
=
Holy, holy, holy,
Lord God Almighty. 
Early in the morning
Our song shall rise to Thee; 
Holy, holy, holy!
Merciful and mighty!
God in Three Persons,
Blessed Trinity!
=
Diyin, Diyinii God,
Ayo’o’’ ánóodziilii, Ts’ídá abínígo
dahwiitaał Diyin, Diyinii God,
Jiiníba’ dóó Nidziil, Táa’go 
T’ááłá’í Náńdlíinii nílį’
=
Holy, holy, holy, 
Though the darkness hide Thee,
Though the eye of sinful man 
Thy glory may not see,
Only Thou art holy; 
There is none beside Thee
Perfect in pow'r,
In love and purity.
=
Diyin, Diyinii God,
Ayó'ánóodziilii,
Iinilaii t'á'át'é naa
Dahaniih dooleeł.
Diyin, Diyinii God,
Jiiníba' dó Nidziil,
Táa'go T'áłá'í
Náłdlíinii nílí̜.
