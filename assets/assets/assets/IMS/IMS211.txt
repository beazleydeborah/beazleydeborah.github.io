Glory, Hallelujah
order:1,2,1;3
=
Sing glory, glory, hallelujah!
All glory be to God above!
Our voices we raise as gladly we praise
Lord of life and love,
Lord of life and love.
=
Now in every land,
Join the angel band,
Telling all of Jesus' birth.
As our hearts unite,
We share His light,
And peace, goodwill on earth,
And Peace, goodwill on earth.
=
Hallelujah, amen!
Hallelujah, amen!
