Great is thy Name
=
Oh, Precious Lord,
Great is Thy name, great is Thy name.
Oh, Precious Lord,
Great is Thy name in all the earth.
Oh, Lamb of God,
Great is Thy name, great is Thy name.
Oh Lamb of God,
Great is Thy name in all the earth.
