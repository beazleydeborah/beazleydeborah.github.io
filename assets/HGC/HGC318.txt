REVELATION 4:8 
topic:scripture
=
Holy, holy, holy 
is the Lord God, Almighty, 
Who was and Who is, and Who is to come.
Glory, glory, glory
to the Lord God, Almighty,
Who died and now lives and Who is to come.
Oh, God fill our hearts
with the love of Jesus Christ, and You, You.
Holy, holy, holy
is the Lord God, Almighty,
Who was and Who is, and Who is to come.
