title:O The Love of My Redeemer
order:1,2,3,4,3,5,6,3
=
Highest highs or deepest deeps
Be there pain or poverty
There is nothing that can 
keep my Redeemers love from me
=
All alone though I may feel 
All this world my enemy 
Still theres no one that can steal 
My Redeemers love from me
=
O the love of My Redeemer
Never failing come what may
He has purchased my forgiveness
And has washed my sins away
=
Although burdened by the weight
Of great trial or tragedy 
None of these can seperate 
My Redeemers love for me
=
Though the earths foundations shake 
Driving wind or raging sea
Neither death nor life can take 
My Redeemers love from me
=
Through this worlds few passing days
And through all eternity 
I will never cease to praise 
My Redeemers love for me
=
