title:Christ Hanásdzá!
subtitle:Christ Is Risen!
language:navajo
=
Christ hanásdzá, baa dohohniih!
Aniné bideeni’ yę́ę́
Yik’eh deesdlį́į’, baa dahohniih!
Baa hózhǫ́ǫgo dahwiitaał.
=
Christ hanás dzá, baa dahohniih!
T’áá bí t’éí bóhólníih doo,
Christ nídoodááł, baa dahohniih!
Nihokáá’ naat’áanii doo.
=
Jesus hiná, baa dahohniih!
Anoonééł yik’eh deesdlį́į́’,
K’ad hool’áágóó hinii’náa doo,
T’áá bí baa dahwii’niih doo.
=
Baa hózhóonii baa dahohniih!
Nihí dó’ biniilt’éego
Hinii’náa doo, baa dahohniih!
Ti’hoo’nííh yę́ę ádin doo.
