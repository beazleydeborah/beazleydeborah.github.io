Deeper and Deeper
=
Draw me from fear into Your faithfulness,
Lead me along Your ways.
Draw me from sin into Your holiness,
That I may be a living praise.
=
Draw me deeper, deeper into Your Word of truth.
Draw me deeper, deeper to my love for You.
Draw me deeper, deeper into the place where You are,
Draw me deeper and deeper that I may know Your heart.
=
Draw me from hate into charity,
From restlessness into Your peace.
Draw me from anger, Lord, into gentleness,
Till ev'ry storm in my life shall cease.
=
Draw me deeper, deeper into Your Word of truth.
Draw me deeper, deeper to my love for You.
Draw me deeper and deeper that I may know Your heart.
