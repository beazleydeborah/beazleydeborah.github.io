He is Holy
=
He is holy, He is holy,
And we praise His holy name.
He is holy, He is holy, He is holy.
=
Exalt the Lord, our God;
Worship at His throne.
Praise His great and awesome name
For the faithfulness He's shown.
=
You are holy, You are holy,
And we praise Your holy name.
You are holy, You are holy, You are holy.
