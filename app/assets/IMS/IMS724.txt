Beautiful Star of Bethlehem
topic:christmas
subtitle:w/Navajo
language:navajo
order:1,2,3,2,4,2
=
Bethlehem-di ei so' ni-zho-niii 
Cha-ha-oh-yee ei a-yool-diil
Yoo-a-da-haas-kai ei ya-a-dool-dlal
Has-toi da-ho-yaann so' bi-K'eh-go
Jesus si-ti-ni-ji' ji'-kai
Bethlehem di so' ni-zhoniii naas goo
=
Bethlehem-di ei (ni-zho-niii ni-zho-niii so)
So ni-zho-niii (Bethlehem-di so')
Ho-di-yin bee ni-hi-k'i-dii diin doo
A-di-ni-diin ni-haa di-ni-aah
Keyah ha-da-hool-t'eii bii'ji'
=
A-di-ni-dlin bee il-cho-hoo-i
De'-e-yo-ni ya-a-doool dlal
Dzil da-bi-tis-goo yoo'ish hoos'iid-ji
A-yoo-a-nool-nin-go a-din-diin
Keyah ha-da-hool-t'eii bii-ji'
Bethlehem di so' ni-zhoniii naas goo
=
Jo so ni-zho-niii bee haa-ii-yiih
Yis-da-haas-kai yi-ki-dzis-dli'
Ya'aash-di ei naa-la-ch'ah yii-doo-biil
Haa-la Jesus ei so di-yi-nii
Ji-lii-go ha-tsa-di-ni-diin
Bethlehem di so' ni-zhoniii naas goo
=
O beautiful star of Bethlehem
Shining far through shadows dim
Giving the light for those who long have gone
Guiding the wise men on their way
Unto the place where Jesus lay
O beautiful star of Bethlehem
Shine on
=
O beautiful star the hope of life
Guiding the pilgrims through the night
Over the mountains 'til the break of dawn
Into the land of perfect day
It will give out a lovely ray
O beautiful star of Bethlehem
Shine on
=
O beautiful star of Bethlehem
Shine upon us until the glory dawns
Give us a lamp to light the way
Unto the land of perfect day
O beautiful star of Bethlehem
Shine on
=
O beautiful star the hope of grace
For the redeemed, the good and the blessed
Yonder in glory when the crown is won
Jesus is now the star divine
Brighter and brighter He will shine
O beautiful star of Bethlehem
Shine on
=
O beautiful star of Bethlehem
Shine upon us until the glory dawns
Give us a lamp to light the way
Unto the land of perfect day
O beautiful star of Bethlehem
Shine on
