Je Louerai l'Eternel
language:french
=
Je louerai l'Eternel (zhu loo ray lay tair nel)
De tout mon coeur, (day toot moan cure)
Je raconterai  (zhu rah con tu ray)
Toutes  tes merveilles,  (toot tay tay mairvyay)
Je chanterai ton  nom.  (zhu shan tairay tone nome)
Je  louerai l'Eternel (zhu loo ray lay tair nel)
De tout mon coeur, (day toot moan cure)
Je ferai de toi  (zhu fairay day twa)
Le sujet de ma joie. (lay soozhay day ma zhwee)
Alleluia!
=
Praise, I will praise you, lord,
With all my heart.
O God, I will tell 
The wonders of your ways,
And glorify your name.
Praise, I will praise you, Lord,
With all my heart.
In you will I find 
The source of all my joy.
