title:Jesus Alááh !nóolnin
subtitle:w/Navajo
language:navajo
=
Fairest Lord Jesus.
Ruler of all nature. 
O Thou of God and man the Son.
Thee will I cherish,
Thee will I honor,
Thou my soul's glory, joy, and crown!
=
T’iis danizhóní,
Hootsonizhóní,
Éí ayóó’ánóolnin nidi,
Christjígo alááh,
Jesus niłtóólí,
Yínííł bii’dę́ę́’ bí bee hashtaał.
=
Fair is the sunshine,
Fairer still the moonlight, 
and all the twinkling starry host:
Jesus shines brighter,
Jesus shines purer 
than all the angels heav'n can boast.
=
Kéyaht’áá’át’é
Beeníhólníihii,
God biye’ dinésilí̜i̜’ii bee
Baa ha’niihii,
Ts’ídá t’áá’át’é
K’ad dóó hool’áágóóbíí’dooleeł.
