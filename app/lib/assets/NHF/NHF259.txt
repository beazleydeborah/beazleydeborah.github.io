title:Under His Wings
order:1,2,3,2,4,2
=
Bit'a biyaa, sih haz'aagi honishlo, 
Chahalheel doo nahonitl'a nidi, 
Ba'iinishliigo 
t'aa ei shaa ahalya,
Naashiis'nii'aadoo be'awee' nishli.
=
Bit'a' biyaa, bit'a' biyaa,
Haisha' yits'a shidoolteel,
Bit'a' biyaa, akwii honishlo, 
Hool'aagoo bil honishlo.
=
Bit'a' biyaa, doolado' baa hodlii da,
Bidin nishliigo ei bee haahashyiih,
Azee' bee naadideeshdaalii ei a din,
Ei bee ha'iinishni, ei shil hosho.
=
Bit'a' biyaa, doolado' il ho shoo da,
T'aa akwii t'ei bine' ahinishcheeh,
Nicho'igii doo atishidooliil da,
Hool'aagoo Jesus ei bee yisdee ya.
